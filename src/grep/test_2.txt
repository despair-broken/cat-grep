Johnny Depp aka John Christopher Depp II is an American actor, producer and
musician. As of 2023, Johnny Depp’s net worth is $210 million. By portraying
Captain Jack Sparrow in Walt Disney’s Pirates of the Caribbean series
(2003–2017).

Depp was married to Amber Heard from 2015 to 2017. Heard’s claims that Depp had
been violent to her during their relationship received public attention during
their divorce. He sued the proprietors of the British newspaper The Sun for
defamation under english law in 2018, claiming that Heard had insulted him.
